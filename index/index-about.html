About us

Princess Sitters wants to help all parents around the nation have a night to themselves while making sure their child is having their needs met, but also having a fun, safe night. Having a Princess Princess come and babysit will change their minds all together about having a babysitter. Our Princesses are fully ceritfied and trained in Child Care, as well as had some princess training courses to be as authentic as possible.

Our Princesses

Our Princesses are fully ceritfied and trained in Child Care, as well as had some princess training courses to be as authentic as possible. They come with hand-made, realistic costumes, hair and make-up

Become a Princess

There are a few requirements to becoming a Princess, but fill out an application and read through carefully our pre-requisites and we woud love to have you as a part of our team. We offer full-time slots, where you can babysit up to 40 hours per week, or part-time working around 20 hours per week. We gather your contacts and connect you to potential clients through our website and you choose who, when and where.
